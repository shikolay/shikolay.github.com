glassave

"Discount per action" platform for Glass that allows:

save money

for customers

get clients

for business

How it works

For customer:

You simply ask your glass: "Ok Glass save money on baying jeans". (Jeans is just example) And you get list of stores where you can get discount for some action (like share photo with new jeans or check in inside shop). You go to shop, make action, get discount, buy jeans. Simple and awesome.

For business:

You want to everebody know that new great product arraved to your shop. Login to glassave.com upload product info, define discount and action (like twitt about your shop or share picture with product). And see how you get a lot of new clients. Simple and awesome.
